WeatherHunt for iOS

WeatherHunt allows you to quickly see hundreds of weather forecasts at a glance so you can find the weather you're seeking. Are you looking to go hiking this weekend but don't want soggy feet? Are you just looking to just get out of the drizzle and overcast? WeatherHunt shows you where the sun is shining!

Technology

WeatherHunt uses data directly from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) for the most up to date and accurate forecasts. Every map view shows a forecast for over a hundred locations so you can quickly see how the weather is looking around you. In other weather apps you may need to save and swipe through countless locations to get an idea of the surrounding weather. WeatherHunt lets you simply swipe and pinch the map to the area you're interested in to see all the weather instantly.
